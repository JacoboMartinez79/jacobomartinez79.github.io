Wizards Day Project
Unity — C#
List of Contributions:
Generalist Programmer
As a generalist programmer on "Wizards Day," I took on a variety of tasks essential to the game's development:
  • Wave Spawner: Designed and implemented a robust wave spawner system that dynamically generated enemy waves with configurable parameters for difficulty, timing, and enemy types. Optimized to handle increasing difficulty levels without compromising performance.
  • Audio: Integrated sound effects and background music to create an immersive audio environment. Developed an in-game audio settings menu allowing players to adjust volume levels for a customizable experience.
  • Leveling System: Implemented an XP drop system where enemies drop experience points on death. Every five levels, players receive boosts such as the ability to sprint, increased damage, and more health.
Project Manager
  • Feature Planning: Identified essential features, broke them down into manageable tasks, and assigned work to team members based on their strengths and expertise.
  • Task Management: Delegated responsibilities, tracked progress, addressed roadblocks, and prioritized critical components to keep the project on schedule.
  • Communication: Conducted regular team meetings to discuss progress, share updates, and keep everyone aligned on goals and priorities.
