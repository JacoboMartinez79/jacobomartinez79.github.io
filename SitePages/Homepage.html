Artificial Intelligence

Engine Development
Implemented a particle system using sorted and unsorted pools. Sorted pools managed active particles for efficient rendering, while unsorted pools handled inactive particles for quick allocation and deallocation. This optimized performance for large numbers of particles with smooth, responsive visual effects.
